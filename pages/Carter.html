Dogs are my World

Carter Weldon

Main Page

My favorite animals are dogs. I know that this is a pretty Generic choice but I just like them. I grew up around dogs all the time and I think that they are a great animal if you just need a friend. I had a very rough childhood and I had a dog named Toby. He was alot bigger than me but he was like a brother to me in a wierd sort of way. I probably wouldnt have made it through the stuff that I did go through without a friend like Toby. Dogs just have a special place in my heart.
